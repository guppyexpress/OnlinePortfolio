About Me

    Keeping my passion for computer programming and for aquariums I discovered the passion for plants, so on top of collecting not only fish I started to also collect plants aswell, so now inside almost every room of my house i have several plants. I have learned that these plants help get my mind off of stress and get me more relaxed from all my constant work.


    Continuing with my life it has not always been so great, I have had to deal with stress and family sickness and hardships. Because of that I have been investing more and more time into Virtual Reality so that I could use that to help avoid much of my past and get away from most of the causes of stress in my day to day living.


    I continue daily to try to improve my living and greatly remove stress from my life. I hope you enjoyed this short biography about me and my life, as my life changes I will continue to add more and more things onto this autobiography to help keep my life story open for others to read and learn from.
